HoHoBot - Your Holiday Discord Bot

Welcome to HoHoBot, your holiday Discord bot! Spread the holiday cheer with our Christmas-themed commands and experience the joy of winter and snow. Whether you're looking for cozy content, Christmas jokes, or checking if it's Christmas, HoHoBot has got you covered!

Available Commands:

  • /christmas - Fetches Christmas Reddit posts
  • /cozy - Discover cozy Reddit posts
  • /joke - Enjoy Christmas jokes
  • /isitchristmas - Checks if it's currently Christmas
